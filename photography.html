Photography

What do we have for you?

Wedding Stories


We prefer to tell the story of your wedding day with as little interference as possible. We have the experience to be in the right place at the right time to capture those incredible gone-in-a-second moments that you may otherwise miss forever and which make each day so unique.

What about the family photos?

What would a wedding be without chasing around the family for a few photos? Our approach to family photography at weddings is simple. We have a suggested list of 3-6 photos for immediate family. We understand that weddings aren’t all the same, so of course there is some variation expected on this list, but our interest is getting the key family “mantel piece” photos in a quick and efficient way so you can get back to the fun of the main event.

Maternity Sessions


Our goal is to capture the beauty that is your pregnant body and to also capture the connection between you and the little soul inside you. We will also celebrate these last moments as a family of two, three, or whatever size your family is right now. I think it is special to have memories of what your family looked like right before the baby arrives. New Born Photo Session


After the birth of your child, we will schedule a time to meet again to capture those sleepy eyes, and tiny baby smiles. It’s not all about the baby; it’s about the love the parents have for the baby as well. Little details are so important to me; I love photographing little fingers, toes and noses of these tiny babes because they’ll only be tiny for so long.

Baby and Toddler Photography Sessions


We are extremely passionate about our baby and toddler photography. Babies grow and develop so quickly! There are so many special moments you can capture, whether is their first smiles, sitting up or, their first steps. Our sessions are fun, relaxed and all about capturing precious time with your baby. We take a whole range of different images during your shoot to ensure we capture your babies character. When you book your session it is really important to work around your Baby’s sleep and feeding times. This will help ensure your baby is at their best.
