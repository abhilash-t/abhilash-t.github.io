From the Blog

Who are we?


At HAPPENING PIXELS, we aim for endless vantage points of capturing memorable images.

We relish the opportunity to capture scenes full of joy, love and even sadness – shared moments, intimate moments, reflective moments. We love the timeless elegance of black and white images, the classic look of film and consistent effort in our work. What could be more important than giving your family and friends beautiful images that will provide joy for years, and hopefully, generations to come?

Happening Pixels is a team of photographers, cinematographers, DOP, creative designers, content writer, marketing team led by Harshitha Venu.

Our Services

Happening Pixels is 'full service' studio.

What does this mean? Well let's start with what we're not. We don't take few photos or capture some footage, burn it to a disc and post it out to you, job done - one of your guests could do that!

We have extensive experience to visualize meticulously plan, organize and execute entire event successfully. A fabulous collection of albums and USB drives, all presented in gorgeous packaging. (hyperlink-hp packaging service).
