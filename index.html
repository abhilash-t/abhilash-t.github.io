At Happening Pixels, we aim for endless vantage points of capturing memorable images.

We relish the opportunity to capture scenes full of joy, love and even sadness – shared moments, intimate moments, reflective moments. We love the timeless elegance of black and white images, the classic look of film and consistent effort in our work. What could be more important than giving your family and friends beautiful images that will provide joy for years, and hopefully, generations to come?
